HomesKitsPro serves a wide range of customers across Kenya. We also serve small businesses and offices equipping their workspaces, contractors and DIY buyers sourcing reliable tools and fittings, and gift buyers looking for practical, high-quality products from our home and electronics range.

No matter your budget or project size, we stock options at different price points to make sure there is something that works for you — without compromising on quality or reliability.

Our Vision

Our vision is to become Kenya's most trusted and comprehensive online home store — the first place customers turn when they need something for their home, without needing to leave the house to find it. We are continuously growing our catalogue to include smart home devices, energy-efficient appliances, solar accessories, and home office solutions, so that HomesKitsPro grows alongside the homes and lifestyles of our customers.

Quality, affordability, and genuine helpfulness are not targets we aim for — they are the standard we operate from every single day. If a product does not meet the standard we would want in our own homes, it does not go on the site. That commitment is what HomesKitsPro is built on, and it is what we intend to stand behind as we grow.
